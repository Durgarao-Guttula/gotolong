Investor Check List

1. Check DER (Debt/Equity Ratio)
2. Check ICR (Interest coverage ratio) - 3 years
3. Check ROCE3
4. Check ROE3
5. Check DPR3 (dividend payout ratio)
6. Check sales5 (sales growth over 5 years)
7. Check profit5 (profit growth over 5 years)
8. Check pledge of promoters shares
9. Check that company is in BSE-500/NSE-500 (top 500 by mcap)
10. The stock price is max +25% of 52-week low and has minimum 20% margin of safety.
